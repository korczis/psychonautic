## Neuralni site

Psychedelika dramaticky ovlivnuji funkcni mozkove site:

| Sit | Funkce | Efekt psychedelik |
|-----|--------|-------------------|
| [Default Mode Network](@/circuits/dmn.md) | Sebereference, rumminace | Dezintegrace, ego dissolution |
| [Salience Network](@/circuits/salience-network.md) | Detekce dulezitosti | Zvysena aktivita |
| [Central Executive](@/circuits/central-executive-network.md) | Kognitnivni kontrola | Snizena integrita |
| [Visual Network](@/circuits/visual-network.md) | Zrakove zpracovani | Halucinace |
| [Dorsal Attention](@/circuits/dorsal-attention-network.md) | Cilena pozornost | Oslabena fokusace |

|---------|--------|
