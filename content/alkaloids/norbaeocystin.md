# Norbaeocystin - 4-Fosforyloxytryptamin

**Norbaeocystin** (4-PO-T, 4-fosforyloxytryptamin) je indolovy alkaloid a **biosynteticky prekurzor** [baeocystinu](@/alkaloids/baeocystin.md) a [psilocybinu](@/alkaloids/psilocybin.md) v psilocybinových houbach. Jedna se o nejrannejsi fosforylovany intermediat v biosynteticke dráze. Psychoaktivita teto latky je dosud malo prozkoumana.

## Biosynteza

### Pozice v biosynteticke draze

Norbaeocystin je **klicovy intermediat** v biosynteticke draze psilocybinu:

```
L-TRYPTOFAN (proteinogenni aminokyselina)
    |
    | [PsiD - tryptofan dekarboxylaza]
    | (odstepenni CO2, kofaktor: PLP)
    v
TRYPTAMIN
    |
    | [PsiH - tryptamin 4-hydroxylaza]
    | (cytochrom P450, O2, NADPH)
    v
4-HYDROXYTRYPTAMIN (4-HT)
    |
    | [PsiK - 4-hydroxytryptamin kinaza]
    | (fosforylace ATP -> ADP)
    v
=========================================
|        NORBAEOCYSTIN (4-PO-T)         |  <-- TATO LATKA
|        (nejrannejsi fosfor. prekurzor) |
=========================================
    |
    | [PsiM - S-adenosylmethionin methyltransferaza]
    | (1. N-metylace, SAM -> SAH)
    v
BAEOCYSTIN (4-PO-NMT)
    |
    | [PsiM - S-adenosylmethionin methyltransferaza]
    | (2. N-metylace, SAM -> SAH)
    v
PSILOCYBIN (4-PO-DMT)
```

### Enzymy podilející se na premene

| Enzym | Gen | Reakce | Kofaktor |
|-------|-----|--------|----------|
| **PsiD** | psiD | Dekarboxylace Trp | PLP (vitamin B6) |
| **PsiH** | psiH | 4-hydroxylace | O2, NADPH, P450 |
| **PsiK** | psiK | Fosforylace 4-HT | ATP |
| **PsiM** | psiM | N-metylace (2x) | SAM |

### Genovy klastr psilocybinu

Biosynteticke geny jsou organizovany v klastru (~25 kbp):

```
                    Genový klastr psilocybinu
    ====================================================
    |  psiD  |  psiH  |  psiK  |  psiM  | regulacni |
    |--------|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
    | 1.4 kb | 1.7 kb | 1.0 kb | 0.9 kb |  ~ 1 kb   |
    ====================================================
           ↓        ↓        ↓        ↓
        TrpDC    P450     Kinaza   MeT
```

### Regulace hromadeni norbaeocystinu

Za urcitych podminek se norbaeocystin muze akumulovat:

| Podminka | Mechanismus | Vysledek |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Limitace SAM** | Nedostatek metyl-donoru | Hromadeni norbaeocystinu |
| **Nizka exprese PsiM** | Geneticka variabilita | Vyssi pomr norbaeocystinu |
| **Mlade mycelium** | Biosyntetický prekurzor | Detekce v rannych fazich |
| **Stres** | Zmenena regulace | Variabilni |

```
    | (hydrolýza fosforylové skupiny)
    v

    | [Distribuce do CNS]

    v
5-HT2A receptor aktivace (?)
    |
    v
Psychoaktivni ucinky (?)
```
